Getting Around

Transportation

Minneapolis prides itself on having many means of transport all around the city and the surrounding suburbs. In downtown everything is in walking distance and during our cold Minnesota winters feel free to take advantage of our extensive skyway system. Just in the past few years we have created a light rail system that will take you to St. Paul, Mall of America and many of the larger suburbs. In the summer Minneapolis also provides bikes and scooters for quick and enviromentally friendly options of getting around the city.

Welcome

As the restrictions due to COVID-19 continue to evolve in Minnesota and in the city of Minneapolis, we want to provide you with the latest information so that you can explore and visit Minneapolis again with confidence.

What to Know

  • Always wear a mask/ face covering and make sure it fits over your nose and mouth
  • Practice safe social distancing. Please keep six feet between yourself and other people
  • Please wash and sanitize your hands very often
  • A limited number of visitors will be permitted in the stores.
  • A one way flow of traffic will be required in the stores and some other areas
  • Contactless payment is available and encouraged throughout the city
